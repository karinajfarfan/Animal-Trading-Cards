Blue glaucus

Glaucus atlanticus was recently found in the Humboldt Current ecosystem in Peru in 2013, and off Andhra Pradesh in India in 2012.

  • Scientific Name: Glaucus atlanticus
  • Average Length: 3 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 1-3 years
  • Habitat: Circumglobal in temperate and tropical waters

The sea slugs can move toward prey or mates by using their cerata to make slow swimming movements. This animal is able to feed on the Portuguese man o' war due to its immunity to the venomous nematocysts. Glaucus is a hermaphrodite, having both male and female reproductive organs.
